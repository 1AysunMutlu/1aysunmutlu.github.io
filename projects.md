## Projects & Research

### 🎯 Promotion Optimization Research
Led a field experiment with a subscription-based service, comparing the effectiveness of various promotional incentives (percentage vs absolute discounts vs loyalty points) on customer purchase behavior.

### 🚂 Rail Track Detection with 3D LiDAR
At CN Railway, I worked on a LiDAR-based segmentation model to identify rail assets using rasterized point cloud data and deep learning.

### 🧠 Reinforcement Learning for Personalized Marketing
Built RL and MILP models to optimize the timing, type, and frequency of marketing offers across heterogeneous customer segments.
