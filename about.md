## About Me

I am a data scientist with a PhD in Operations Management from McGill University. My work bridges behavioral research with machine learning, combining experimental methods and optimization models.

I’ve conducted real-world experiments with leading companies, focusing on how different promotion types influence customer behavior over time. My current interests include reinforcement learning for customer retention, digital twins in retail, and explainable AI.

Beyond research, I love photography, travel, and sharing stories about the academic journey.
