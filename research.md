### 🎯 Promotion Optimization Research
Led a randomized field experiment with a subscription-based company. Compared percentage discounts, absolute discounts, and loyalty points. Developed MILP + RL models for prescriptive allocation.

### 🚂 Rail Detection via 3D LiDAR
Worked at CN Railway on semantic segmentation of LiDAR point cloud for rail track detection. Applied FCN on rasterized elevation maps.

### 🤖 RL for Personalized Marketing
Built and tested a reinforcement learning-based promotion policy using heterogeneous treatment effects and conversion predictions.
